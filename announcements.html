Announcements

We hope that you have a pleasant and safe trip to Vienna and wish to request that you please take a few minutes to read the following useful information.

Before travelling, make sure if you need a visa to come to Vienna and that you are carrying a valid passport.

DO NOT ride a taxi outside the airport. There are several taxi companies which you can hire before leaving Immigration. Stay tuned for updates on affordable local transport.

HOTELS:

  • The Congress has special rates on 3 hotels but you need to book them through our web page when you register
  • We will have return transportation from these hotels to the Congress site (PUCP’s campus) and we will provide timetables and schedules.
  • When arriving at the hotel, hand your passport to the receptionist. They need to photocopy it so that you can get the international visitor’s tax exemption.

WEATHER:

  • During the month of July, temperatures in Austria reach between 81°F to 61°F (27ºC - 18ºC.). You might need to bring clothes to keep cool because it can also get humid.

HEALTH:

  • Please stay hydrated and drink only bottled water as tap water is not recommended.

ATTIRE:

  • Casual clothes during the meeting.

TIPPING:

  • Most restaurants include a 10% tip. The total should be around 15%. Doorkeepers should be tipped USD$ 2 per bag. Taxi drivers do not expect a tip.

HANDICRAFTS:

  • Austrian handicrafts such as fine textiles, paintings, jewelry and pottery can be found in shops near the hotels.

GASTRONOMY:

  • Vienna is very well known for its great food. Although Austrian food may be a little different than American, it is very tasty. Don’t miss trying its renowned plates.

CUSTOMS:

  • NO objects of archaeological, historical or artistic value may be taken out of the country unless you carry the proper permit. Austria has international agreements with most countries for the confiscation and the return of the objects which have been taken abroad

SECURITY ADVISE:

  • Vienna shares the same problems which plague any large city but if you use your common sense and have a little precaution you can avoid being a victim of any incident.
  • We suggest you leave your passport, airticket and valuables in the hotel´s safety box. Always carry a photocopy of your passport.
  • At the Airport, please keep your luggage at sight. Taxis in Vienna have no taximeters. Make sure to negotiate an amount before you take one.
